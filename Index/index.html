What is EDUCARTA?

Today almost industries and service areas are rapidly turning in to Information Technology based systems. But there is a significant need of IT based systems in educational sector. Academic Performance Management System is a supportive application to students as well as lectures to enhance their academic performance. Really this software acts as a bridge between lectures and students, a doorway to interact between those two entities easily.


Why EDUCARTA?

EDUCARTA will improve efficiency and productivity in the Education process. Educarta provides lots of benefits for creating, distributing, collecting, analyzing, and releasing reports of feedback forms. Feedback forms are key area of Educarta system. Educarta system turns manual feedback collecting system into efficient and effective computerize system. It will save more money, more time as well as it is eco-friendlier than manual system of collecting feedbacks. Educarta can release results and can turn data into information very effectively. So results can be used to development processes and also evaluations processes of lecturer’s performance evaluation system. There will be generating a special report about students who don’t submit the feedback forms. It helps to motivate students to submit the feedback forms. Everyone get freedom to ask questions without disturbing the flow of lecture and also they can get rid of negative motivation from other students. Because Educarta protects privacy of the student who ask questions. So there will be questions at Q&A sessions.
